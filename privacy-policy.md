## Introduction

KnowledgeBase ("we", "our", or "us") operates the KnowledgeBase mobile application and website ({{ site.url }}).

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

## Information Collection and Use

### Data We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.** Our application is designed with privacy in mind:

- **No account required** - You can use the app without creating an account
- **No analytics tracking** - We don't use Google Analytics or similar services
- **No advertising** - We don't show ads or share data with advertisers
- **Local storage only** - Your preferences (theme, favorites) are stored locally on your device

### Local Storage

The app uses your device's local storage to save:
- Theme preference (dark/light mode)
- Favorite articles list
- Language preference

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any server.

### Third-Party Services

Our app may contain links to external websites (GitHub, LinkedIn, YouTube, arXiv). These third-party sites have their own privacy policies, and we have no responsibility for their content or activities.

## Cookies

We do not use cookies for tracking. The only cookies used are:
- Google Translate functionality (optional, user-initiated)

## Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
